Meet Venky

With over 7 years of experience and more than 600 weddings captured, Venky has established V Creations as a trusted name in luxury event photography across Telangana.

Specializing in wedding photography, bespoke album design, and professional drone cinematography — Venky brings a rare combination of artistic vision and technical mastery to every celebration.

Based in Huzur Nagar, Telangana, V Creations is dedicated to preserving your most sacred moments with warmth, creativity, and unmatched attention to detail.
